FAQ

Everything About
MCS-P Swap

All your questions about swapping and staking on MCS-P Swap at mcs-p-swap.vercel.app, answered.

What is MCS-P Swap?+
MCS-P Swap (mcs-p-swap.vercel.app) is a TRON-native DEX aggregator and staking platform built around the MCSP token. It finds the best TRC20 token swap routes across all major TRON liquidity pools including SunSwap, JustSwap, SunStable and more — all in a single, non-custodial transaction. MCS-P Swap also offers a staking module where users earn up to 48% APY in MCSP rewards.
What is the MCSP token?+
MCSP is the native utility and governance token of the MCS-P Swap ecosystem, issued on TRON as a TRC20 token. MCSP holders benefit from reduced swap fees, staking rewards, and governance voting rights over protocol upgrades. The token is tradeable on MCS-P Swap and all major TRON DEXes directly at mcs-p-swap.vercel.app.
How do I swap tokens on MCS-P Swap?+
Visit mcs-p-swap.vercel.app and connect your TronLink wallet using the "Connect Wallet" button. Select your input TRC20 token (e.g., USDT) and your output token (e.g., MCSP). Review the quoted rate, estimated output, and route. Click "Swap Now" and approve the transaction in TronLink. Your swapped tokens arrive in under 3 seconds thanks to TRON's fast finality.
How do I stake MCSP and earn rewards?+
Navigate to the Staking section on MCS-P Swap. Connect your TronLink wallet, enter the amount of MCSP you wish to stake, choose a lock period (7 days at 9% APY up to 365 days at 48% APY), review the reward preview and unlock date, then click "Stake MCSP". Rewards are distributed daily directly to your connected wallet and can be claimed at any time without interrupting your staking position.
What is the maximum APY for staking MCSP?+
The maximum APY on MCS-P Swap is 48%, available for the 365-day MCSP Platinum staking pool. Shorter periods offer 9% (7 days), 22% (30 days), and 36% (90 days). All rewards are paid in MCSP tokens and distributed daily to stakers. The longer you commit, the more you earn per day.
What fees does MCS-P Swap charge?+
MCS-P Swap charges a protocol fee of 0.05% on swaps — one of the lowest rates on TRON. MCSP token holders receive an additional 20% fee discount, reducing their fee to just 0.04%. Network bandwidth costs on TRON are negligible, often under $0.001. There are zero hidden charges; the displayed quote always includes all fees before you confirm.
Is MCS-P Swap safe and audited?+
Yes. MCS-P Swap is fully non-custodial — your funds never leave your wallet until a swap executes atomically on TRON. All smart contracts have been audited by reputable security firms. MEV protection is built in to shield your transactions from front-running bots. If a swap cannot complete for any reason, tokens are automatically returned to your wallet without any loss.
What wallets are compatible with MCS-P Swap?+
MCS-P Swap is optimized for TronLink (browser extension and mobile app), which is recommended for the best TRON experience. It also supports TokenPocket, Trust Wallet (TRON network), WalletConnect-compatible TRON wallets, and imToken. No account or email registration is required — just connect your wallet at mcs-p-swap.vercel.app and start swapping or staking immediately.
Does MCS-P Swap require KYC?+
No. MCS-P Swap is a fully decentralized protocol. There is no account registration, no email, no identity verification (KYC), and no personal data collection of any kind. All you need is a compatible TRON wallet such as TronLink. Your wallet address is the only identity on-chain, ensuring complete privacy.
How does MCS-P Swap find the best swap rate?+
MCS-P Swap's routing engine simultaneously queries 140+ TRON liquidity pools including SunSwap V1/V2, JustSwap, SunStable, and all other active TRON DEX protocols. A pathfinding algorithm then splits your order optimally across pools to minimize price impact and maximize output. Quotes refresh every 5 seconds so you always act on live, accurate pricing on mcs-p-swap.vercel.app.
Can I unstake my MCSP early?+
Early unstaking is possible but incurs a penalty fee to protect long-term stakers. The penalty ranges from 5% (for the 7-day pool) to 15% (for the 365-day Platinum pool) of your staked principal. Accrued rewards up to the exit point are still claimable. The Flex pool (no lock-up) can be exited at any time with no penalty whatsoever — ideal for those wanting maximum flexibility.
What is slippage and how should I configure it on MCS-P Swap?+
Slippage is the acceptable gap between your quoted price and the final executed price, caused by market movement in the time between quote and confirmation. For stablecoin-to-stablecoin swaps (e.g., USDT to USDD), 0.1% is sufficient. For MCSP or other volatile tokens, 0.5% is recommended. For large orders during high volatility, use 1%. The "Auto" setting uses MCS-P Swap's volatility model to pick the optimal tolerance automatically.
What is MEV protection and why does MCS-P Swap include it?+
MEV (Maximal Extractable Value) bots watch public mempools and front-run swap transactions, forcing you to pay a higher price and extracting value from your trade. MCS-P Swap routes all transactions through a private relay layer on TRON so bots cannot detect and front-run your swaps. This protection is active on every swap at mcs-p-swap.vercel.app with zero extra steps required from you.
Does MCS-P Swap support cross-chain swaps?+
Yes. While MCS-P Swap is primarily optimized for TRON-native TRC20 swaps, it also supports cross-chain routes to and from Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, Arbitrum, Solana and more via integrated bridge protocols. A cross-chain swap automatically handles the source chain swap, bridge transfer, and destination chain swap in a single user action, though it takes slightly longer than native TRON swaps.
How are staking rewards calculated and distributed?+
MCSP staking rewards are calculated on a per-block basis using the stated APY for your pool tier. Every 24 hours, the protocol snapshot distributes earned MCSP rewards to all active stakers proportional to their stake. Rewards accumulate in a claimable balance visible in your staking dashboard on mcs-p-swap.vercel.app, which you can withdraw to your wallet at any time without affecting your staked principal.
Can I get rate quotes without connecting a wallet?+
Yes. You can enter any token pair and amount on MCS-P Swap to see live rate quotes, routing breakdown, price impact estimates, and fee previews without connecting a wallet at all. Wallet connection is only required when you are ready to execute the swap or stake your tokens. This lets you compare rates freely before committing any funds.
What TRC20 tokens can I swap on MCS-P Swap?+
MCS-P Swap supports all TRC20 tokens with active liquidity on TRON, including MCSP, USDT (TRC20), USDD, SUN, JST, WIN, BTT, USDJ, and hundreds more. Any TRC20 token listed on a TRON DEX is automatically discoverable on MCS-P Swap. If you have a contract address for a token not in our default list, you can paste it directly into the token search field.
Is there a MCS-P Swap mobile app?+
MCS-P Swap runs as a fully responsive web application at mcs-p-swap.vercel.app and works seamlessly in TronLink's built-in browser on both iOS and Android. You can also access it via TokenPocket's dApp browser. A dedicated native mobile app is in development and will be announced on our official community channels when ready.
How does MCS-P Swap compare to SunSwap?+
MCS-P Swap is an aggregator that routes through SunSwap (and all other TRON DEXes), meaning you get the best of every liquidity source in one place. Trading directly on SunSwap limits you to their pools only. MCS-P Swap also adds MEV protection, multi-hop routing, cross-chain bridges, and an integrated MCSP staking module — features SunSwap does not offer. Users of mcs-p-swap.vercel.app always get a better or equal rate.
How can I contact MCS-P Swap support?+
Community and support are available through our Telegram group, Discord server, and Twitter/X page (links in the footer). For technical issues or bug reports, use the GitHub repository or the Bug Bounty program. Response times are typically under 4 hours on Telegram for urgent swap issues. Note that MCS-P Swap team members will never DM you first or ask for your private keys — beware of impersonators.
